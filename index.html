Trojan Hedge Fund Group

Introduction

The Trojan Hedge Fund Group is a selective group of students who share a passion for financial markets and a strong interest in the hedge fund industry. The group meets weekly to discuss the previous week's events and potential future events/hypothetical investment plays. The group educates members on various investment strategies such as global macro, distressed, value, event driven, activist, etc

Activities
Upcoming Activities

Activity #1: E-board introduces themselves, presents agenda for semester, overview of hedge funds.

Objective: Understand what a hedge fund is, who e-board is, what organization will be doing

Activity #2: Read Paul Tudor Jones portion from Market Wizards outside of class, watch Trader the Documentary on Paul Tudor Jones video in class together

Objective: Understand Paul Tudor Jones’s understanding of financial markets, futures trading, get excited about hedge funds

Activity #3: Read Principles by Ray Dalio outside of class. Watch Ray Dalio’s "How The Economic Machine Works" in class.

Objective: Understand Ray Dalio’s understanding of financial markets, learn a bit about macro.

Activity #4: Read Beating the Street or One Up On Wall Street outside of class, watch Peter Lynch on "How to Pick Stocks" in class.

Objective: Understand Peter Lynch’s stock picking strategy

Activity #5: Read Reminiscences of a Stock Operator outside of class, watch video on Black Wednesday/Soros speculation

Objective: Understand crowd phycology, market timing, other general timeless investment/speculative lessons.

Activity #6: Read The Outsiders: Eight Unconventional CEOs and Their Radically Rational Blueprint for Success (recommended by Ackman & Buffet) outside of class, watch activist videos in class

Objective: Understand what makes a good executive to make for better understanding of activist investing.

Activity #7: Read or listen to Alchemy of Finance by George Soros outside of class and watch his lecture series on General Theory of Reflexivity outside of class, continue watching his lecture series on Financial Markets in class.

Objective: Understand George Soros’s understanding of financial markets

Activity #8: Read Distressed Debt Analysis: Strategies for Speculative Investors outside of class (or at least some articles, books, or some other material on distressed investing), have experienced distressed debt professional present in class

Objective: Have an understanding of distressed debt investing.
